---

## Phase gates

| Gate | When | Pass condition | If fail |
|---|---|---|---|
| Angle validation | Day 30 | ≥30 activated learners + warm feedback | Re-test positioning before scaling |
| Channel focus | Day 60 | ≥200 activated; one clear winning channel | Reassess which channels you're in |
| Systematize | Day 90 | ≥500 activated; 8 features shipped from feedback | Decide: pivot, persevere, or pause |
